How to Use "I'm Free Tonight"

This app lets you quickly share when you're available and what you're interested in doing, so friends can easily coordinateâ€”no matter where they are or what time zone they're in.

Setting Your Status

  • Enter your name in the top box.
  • Click "Show more options" to reveal advanced settings.
  • Optionally, specify when you'll be free ("Free in...") and for how long ("Available for...").
    This helps friends know exactly when you'll be available, and the app will show a live countdown. All times are relative to the current moment, so it's easy to coordinate across time zones.
  • Optionally, add an activity (e.g., "Dinner", "Board games", "Up for anything!").
  • Click "I'm Free!" or press enter to share your status.
Tip: You can update or remove your status at any time. Your entry will update live for everyone viewing the page.

How Others See You

  • Your name, activity, and availability will appear in the list for everyone to see.
  • The timer will count down live, so friends always know when you're available.
  • If you specify an activity, it will be shown next to your name.
Warning: This is a completely open site.

Anyone can see and edit any entry at any time. Please don't rely on this site for anything important or private. It's meant for a small, trusted group of friends. Don't mess with other people's entries too much ;-)
